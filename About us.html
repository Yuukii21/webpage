Highland Bali Villas Resort and Spa is the first Balinese-inspired destination resort, with high-end facilities and services, in the province of Nueva Ecija, north of Manila, Philippines. Hence recognized as “Bali of the North”, with facilities meticulously inspired by Balinese Architecture, and services which showcase a fusion of Balinese unique culture and the tradition of warm hospitality our country is known for.
